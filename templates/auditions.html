Welcome to MIT!
Here’s your chance to join our family (upperclassmen, transfer students, and graduate students are also welcome)! Information about our audition process for the Fall 2018 cycle is below:
  • WHEN: Satuday 9/1, 9AM-8PM (it only takes ~10 minutes!)
  • WHERE: 4-162
  • SIGN UP: Sign up for a time slot here. E-mail syncopasian@mit.edu if you have a time conflict with auditions. We accept walk-ins, but you may have to wait for an open time slot.
  • AUDITION PROCESS: Please prepare a verse and chorus from a song of your choice (English or any language is fine). Along with this song, you will be asked to complete a few warm-up techniques and a sight-singing excerpt.

A few words of advice:
  • Pick a song that suits your singing style and shows off what you can do–DON’T pick a song just because you think we’ll like it. If you’re into your song, we will be too!
  • “But I don’t speak Chinese/Japanese/Korean?” – being Asian or speaking an Asian language is definitely NOT a requirement.
  • Final word of advice from the crusty senior who is in charge of this webpage: Just relax :) if you're nervous about the audition or anything at all, reach out to some of the current syncos! We don't bite and we're so excited to hear you sing.

Can't wait to meet all of you!! <3
